Hip- Hop Influences

We want to give a special shout- out to the artists that we look up to both in the present and in the past. These artists are a constant inspiration for our creative outlets and we would like to highlight some our favourite work from each of them. Refer to the CURRENT LIST WE FUCK WITH? page for our up and coming favourites

J- Cole

Representing North Carolina, Jermaine Cole has proven that he is a voice to pay attention to as A- List rappers are always up to date on his latest verses and the quality of his content. He has chosen to stay true to his core values throughout his career and it has helped him excel in music. His raw lyrical talent mixed with poetic flow are reasons why he takes up a good chunk of the BMC music library.
